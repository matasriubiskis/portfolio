about

I build products where AI, robotics, and messy physical-world problems meet.

Today I'm a Senior Product Manager at Instacart, leading edge AI, sensor fusion, and computer vision for Caper — the AI-powered shopping cart deployed across the US, Australia, and Europe. Before that, I was Head of Product at Ocado Technology, where I scaled On-Grid Robot Pick from prototype to a robotic picking system in full production.

Before the robots came hardware. I led several IoT products at ecobee, and before that co-founded RockMass Technologies — a mining-tech startup that graduated from HAX, the hardware accelerator in Shenzhen.

On the side, I make natural wine with friends through Bon Sang!, teach product management at BrainStation, and sit on the board of a Canadian non-profit managing land for conservation, Scouting, and Lithuanian heritage.

Two goals in life: make cool things, and learn as much as I can. So far, I'm pretty proud of my progress on both fronts.
